About BlockIt

BlockIt is an easy to use time management system created to make planning easy and enjoyable.

Using the Timeblocking concept, BlockIt makes it easy for users to integrate their tasks into their calendar.
No need to write To-Do-List and never get anything done.
Thanks to BlockIt's scheduling option users are encouraged to schedule their tasks.

How to use BlockIt

After registering you will have your personal Timeblocklist to store your tasks

After clicking on "New Timeblock" you have the option to give your new task a name, a description, a duration and a color-tag.

Outlook into the Future

BlockIt is currently still under development. Features you can expect in the future:

    Drag and Drop Function to move Calendar items

    Option to edit Calendar items from the calendar

    Create projects and add Timeblocks to your projects
